Beefy Fritos Burrito

Description

Only for the real fast food lovers, this is the improved beefy fritos burrito recipe.

Originally a 1$ menu item, this is the improved version with better flavour

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Nacho cheese sauce
  • beef, chicken or steak slices
  • Fritos
  • Beans
  • shredded cheese
  • rice
  • hot sauce

Steps

  1. Get the the beef/chicken/steak slices, beans and rice ready.
  2. Lay the tortillas and top them with the beef/chicken/steak slices, beans and rice.
  3. Fold the tortillas.
  4. Microwave the burritos for 1 to 2 minutes on full power.
  5. Add the Fritos chips, shredded cheese and hot sauce to the burritos.
  6. Enjoy!
