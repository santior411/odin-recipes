Double Down

Description

Why use buns when you can use fried chicken instead!

This burger uses chicken instead of buns to stuff the delicious crispy bacon, monterrey Jack Cheese and BBQ sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • BBQ sauce
  • Monterrey Jack Cheese
