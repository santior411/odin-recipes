Monster Burger

Description

Dare to try this monstruosity of a burger which consists of pink-iced doughnut buns, bacon, fries and mac and cheese!

The starting price of this abomination is $130 and it is intended to be shared by six people.

Ingredients

  • 2 pink-iced doughnut buns
  • Lots of bacon
  • diamond-cut fries
  • mac and cheese

Steps

  1. fry the bacon
  2. Get the mac and cheese ready
  3. Get the diamond fries ready
  4. warm the doughnut buns a little bit
  5. Mix all the ingredients together
  6. Enjoy!
